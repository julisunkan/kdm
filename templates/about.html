Understanding Keyword Scores

Difficulty 0-100 (Lower is Better)
  • 0-30 Easy to rank
  • 31-60 Moderate competition
  • 61-100 High competition
Profitability 0-100 (Higher is Better)
  • 70-100 High potential
  • 40-69 Moderate potential
  • 0-39 Low potential
Opportunity Combined Score
  • High Low difficulty + High profit
  • Medium Balanced risk/reward
  • Low High difficulty or low profit
